About Us

Enyew Agro Industry is one of the industry that located in Ethiopia. It mainly focus on Agriculture and their products.In our country Agriculture is the blood vessel for the country economy and our company have a plan to modernize our agricultural habit and it creates an opportunity for the farmers to get best fertilizers for their products not only farmers but also the Citizens that live in urban place can access the agricultural products with out moving to the markets by online system using our company websites. People get different options to satisfy their want. The enhancement of agricultural value chains and food systems is a central aspect of inclusive and sustainable industrialization. There are many special services that our company gives such as Job Opportunities.

Our Mission and Vision

Enyew Agro Industry is one of the industry that help the people to get agricultural products in simple manner and to create and supply agricultural products for end-consumption. Agricultural products are naturally produced resources for human consumption or other uses. The products require a variety of sectors and industries to support the producers, Our company have a plan to expand consumers' choices and create incentives for farmers. Well-functioning markets and trade enable the optimal allocation of resources, diffuse knowledge and technologies..
